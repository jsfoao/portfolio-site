About

I enjoy creating tools and systems that make development smoother and more scalable for everyone involved. I consider myself a strong team player and value clear communication and collaboration.


I'm naturally curious and always looking to learn something new, whether that means exploring new technologies, experimenting with systems, or picking up a new engine or programming language. I usually have a side project where I can dive deeper into something new.
