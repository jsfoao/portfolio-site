## Overview

I contributed through all three years of development of *The Midnight Walk*, a hybrid flatscreen/VR title released across PS5, PSVR2, and PC (Steam and SteamVR). I worked extensively across both VR and flatscreen experiences, with ownership of core gameplay systems and VR-specific features.

For the past few months, I've also contributed to the Nintendo Switch 2 port, working alongside my primary responsibilities.

## Highlights
* Contributed to shipping a hybrid game from start to finish
* Owned development of the VR experience across PSVR2 and PCVR
* Ensured milestones and certification requirements were met
* Developed foundational gameplay systems used across the entire game  

## Features
### Hybrid Player Controller
Designed and implemented a hybrid VR player controller supporting room-scale and virtual input. Supports many comfort features as expected according to VR games standards (movement tuning, movement directon forward, vignette settings, etc). In addition, solved VR-specific challenges such as head and body clipping and many other edge cases

### Core Gameplay Systems
Designed and developed foundational gameplay systems used across the entire game like interaction, narrative, UI, etc. Worked closely with design to ensure systems were flexible and production-ready.
* Interaction system with a context based approach where each interactable can be extended and defined with modules.
* Node based narrative and quest system
* UI system that extends Unreal's navigation and focus more reliably. Also features a stack system and primary layout architecture similar to how Unreal's Common UI now uses.
