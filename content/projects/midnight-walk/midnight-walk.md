## Overview

I contributed through all three years of development of *The Midnight Walk*, a hybrid flatscreen/VR title released across PS5, PSVR2, and PC (Steam and SteamVR). I worked extensively across both VR and flatscreen experiences, with ownership of core gameplay systems and VR-specific features.

For the past few months, I've also contributed to the Nintendo Switch 2 port, working alongside my primary responsibilities.

## Highlights
* Contributed to shipping a hybrid game from start to finish
* Owned development of the VR experience across PSVR2 and PCVR
* Ensured milestones and certification requirements were met
* Developed foundational gameplay systems used across the entire game 

## Milestones and Certification
Worked closely with the QA and production teams to ensure all certification requirements were met for all platforms with special focus to PSVR2. This involved regular reviews of certification checklists and addressing any issues identified during testing. I also contributed to milestone planning and execution, ensuring that development goals were met on time and that the game was in a good state for each milestone submission.

## Core Gameplay Systems
Designed and developed foundational gameplay systems used across the entire game like interaction, narrative, UI, etc. Worked closely with design to ensure systems were flexible and production-ready. Considering this was the company's first project, ensured all systems were reusable and scalable to support future *Moonhood* projects.
* Hybrid Player Controller supporting both room-scale and virtual input, with comfort features and VR-specific solutions.
* Interaction system with a context based approach where each interactable can be extended and defined with modules/fragments.
* Node based narrative and quest system
* UI system that extends Unreal's navigation and focus more reliably. Also features a stack system and primary layout architecture similar to how Unreal's Common UI now uses.
